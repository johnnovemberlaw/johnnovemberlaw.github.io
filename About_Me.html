About Me

Born & raised in Atlantic Beach, John has returned home to serve our community.

After receiving an International Baccalaureate degree from Stanton College Prep in Jacksonville, John graduated Magna Cumme Laude from the University of Florida. While attending UF Law, John received an award for being the top student in UF Law’s Conservation Clinic and received a fellowship in the Florida Keys after graduation. John has successfully drafted and lobbied for public interest legislation in both Tallahassee and Washington DC and has now opened his practice to create positive change for individuals and our community as a whole.


In addition to being the President of November Law, John is the Executive Director of the veterans support nonprofit organization Gratitude America and is the Executive Director of Outreach for a conservation and education nonprofit organization called COAST. John also acts as a Marketing Manager for US Patriot Title Services.


In addition to being a zealous advocate for critical issues, John enjoys paddle boarding, playing sports, supporting the Jaguars and the Armada, spending time with his family, and riding his bike all over the Beaches.
